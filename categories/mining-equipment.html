Mining Equipment

Sitram Inc. supplies superior quality replacement parts for heavy equipment used in the mining and construction industries. Our products are designed and manufactured specifically for use in demanding mining and construction applications. Our replacement parts are designed and manufactured to meet or exceed the specifications of the original equipment manufacturer backed by our exceptional warranty program.

We are able to supply our replacement parts with much shorter delivery time without compromising our competitive prices and warranty conditions compared with the options available in the market. We focus our attention on replacement parts for Komatsu – P&H and Caterpillar – Bucyrus equipment. We specialize in supplying replacement parts for surface mining equipment such as electric rope shovels, blast-hole drills and draglines.
