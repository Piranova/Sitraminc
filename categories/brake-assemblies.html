Brake Assemblies

We supply high performance brake assemblies for heavy equipment for surface mining operations.

Our line of brakes are designed and manufactured specifically for use in demanding mining applications. Our products are designed and manufactured to meet or exceed the specifications of the original equipment manufacturer backed by our exceptional warranty program.

For electric rope mining shovels and draglines, we supply spring-set/pneumatic disc brakes for hoist, swing, propel, crowd and drag motors.

We are able to supply our brake assemblies with much shorter delivery time without compromising our competitive prices and warranty conditions compared with the options available in the market.

We supply complete brake assemblies and all their replacement parts for the following heavy equipment brands:

  • P&H shovels and draglines
  • Bucyrus/CAT shovels & draglines.
