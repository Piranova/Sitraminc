Patchcords

Product Details and Options

  • We supply fibre optic patch cords and pigtails with industry standard connectors and with UPC and APC polishing.
  • We carry a large inventory of popular patch cords in numerous lengths.
  • We also supply patch cords and pigtails with custom lengths and connectors.
  • If you cannot find the product you are looking for or require a large order, please contact us and we will be pleased to help you.
  • Our warehouse inventory is extensive and allows us to provide products in a short time frame.
  • We also supply custom made cable assemblies including ribbon and fan out patch cord and pigtail assemblies.

Features & Specifications

  • All of our patch cords and pigtails are supplied with Corning fibres.
  • Our patch cords are manufactured in an ISO 9001 environment and all products meet Telcordia GR 326 CORE standard.
  • Each patch cord is tested and supplied with a traceable test report.
